Biography of Jefferson Regacho

Hello, my name is Jefferson Regacho. I was born and raised in Bulacan, and have lived here for 20 years. I attended First City Providential College and graduated in 2018.


After high school, I went on to study Information Technology at First City Providential College. During my time at First City Providential College, I was involved in Community Outreach Programs to help people gain more knowledge about computer and technologies, and striving to graduate with Latin Honors in 2025.


Since then, I have been working as a Freelance Developer at my own business. In my free time, I enjoy selling people apps that helps them in their everyday life.


Thank you for taking the time to read my biography.  If you have any questions or would like to learn more about me, please feel free to contact me at jefferson.regacho@fcpc.com.ph.
